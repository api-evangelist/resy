Somssi is a new Greenwich Village bistro from the team behind acclaimed Korean restaurants Atoboy and Atomix, led by former director of operations Ahris Kim. The restaurant blends Asian sensibilities with classic bistro fare in a warmly designed space, featuring dishes like linguine with mustard kimchi ragu and potato with caviar, alongside curated wine and sake selections.
