Maison Bar a Vins in Washington D.C.'s Adams Morgan neighborhood is a French-inspired wine bar and restaurant operated by chef Matt Conroy and co-owner Omar Popal of The Popal Group. Located in a historic brownstone featuring Victorian fireplaces and imported chandeliers, the restaurant serves classic French cuisine including housemade pasta and brioche-stuffed chicken. The establishment has been nominated for a James Beard Award for Best New Restaurant, demonstrating its significance beyond its wine bar classification.
