Evil Cooks, a restaurant in El Sereno run by chefs Alex and Elvia Garcia, combines heavy metal and punk aesthetics with inventive cooking techniques. The couple transforms culinary traditions through unconventional methods like trompos and cotton candy machines to create dishes with unexpected flavor combinations. Their menu features creatively named items inspired by metal bands and personal memories, reflecting their philosophy of breaking culinary rules while maintaining intentional flavors.
